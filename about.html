Our Story

Empowering Students Through Professional Beauty Services

Founded by IIUM Students

Kaisara BeautyClub was started by two sisters, passionate about providing quality beauty services to the IIUM community. Inspired by their mother's work at GiatMara Malaysia, they combined their skills and entrepreneurial spirit to create this unique service.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality beauty services
  • Support student entrepreneurship
  • Promote self-care within Islamic principles
  • Create a comfortable and respectful service environment

Islamic Principles

We are committed to delivering services that align with Islamic values of modesty, cleanliness, and ethical practice. Our services respect personal dignity and spiritual well-being.
